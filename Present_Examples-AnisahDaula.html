Types of Motherboards

There are a couple different types of motherboards. They have all different sizes, which are known as "Form Factors".

  • micro-ATX (µATX)
  • Mini-ATX
  • FlexATX
  • EATX
  • WATX
  • Nano-ATX
  • Pico-ATX
  • MobileATX

Another smaller form factor is ITX. Which comes from:

  • mini-ITX
  • nano-ITX
  • pico-ITX

Specification of the component and how to choose the right one:

There's never a certain universal answer to this. To pick the correct motherboard, you have to choose based on the PC that you're planning on building or buying. You must choose what works best for the use of the PC based on many factors like, the number of components you want to configure, then you can decide which motherboard best suits your needs. preferences and requirements differ from user to user, so none of the three mainstream motherboard formats can be the best choice for everyone.
